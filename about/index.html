About

My name is Adon Rosen, a student of both biology and psychology. I am interested in finding the synthesis of these topics and their relation to behavior. I graduated from Virginia Tech where I began working with neuroimaging in the labs of both Anthony Cate and Read Montague. Seeing the application of fMRI in both cognitive psychology and computational psychiatry excited my interest in this field. I now work under Ruben Gur combining large scale neuroimaging data with statistical prediction and classification tools with the goal of further unmasking the relationship between the brain and behavior.

I currently live in Philadelphia PA. Philadelphia has allowed me a lot of opportunities to explore cultural and intellectual outlets. Another benefit to being in Philly is that it has given me a lot of access to explore active hobbies such as:

  • Cycling
  • Crossfit
  • Soccer

I am also an avid fan of traveling, cooking, and music. I will be using my blog to document these hobbies, and interests as well as other miscellaneous topics.
